Salamence

Salamence is a Dragon/Flying-type Pokémon from Generation III. It evolves from Shelgon and finally gains wings, letting it fly at high speed. It’s known for a fierce temper and powerful aerial attacks.

  • National Number: #0373
  • Type: Dragon / Flying
  • Species: Dragon Pokémon
  • Height: 1.5 m (4′11″)
  • Weight: 102.6 kg (226.2 lb)
  • Abilities: Intimidate (Hidden: Moxie)
