Salamance

Salamance is a large, dragon-like Pokémon known for its powerful wings and fierce appearance. It has a blue body with a cream-colored underbelly, and its wings are red with a black outline. Salamance is known for its strength and agility in battle, making it a formidable opponent. It evolves from Shelgon starting at level 50, which in turn evolves from Bagon starting at level 30. Salamance is a dual-type Dragon/Flying Pokémon and is often associated with the ability to fly at high speeds and unleash powerful attacks.

  • National Number: #0373
  • Type: Dragon / Flying
  • Species: Dragon Pokémon
  • Height: 1.5 m (4′11″)
  • Weight: 102.6 kg (226.2 lbs)
  • Abilities: Intimidate, Hidden: Moxie
